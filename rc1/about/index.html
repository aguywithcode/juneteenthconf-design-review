About JuneteenthConf

Celebrating and elevating Black excellence in technology since 2020.

JuneteenthConf is a nonprofit organization creating space for Black technologists to connect, learn, and grow. What started as a two-week sprint in 2020 has become a movement — three programs, thousands of community members, and a permanent seat at the table.
