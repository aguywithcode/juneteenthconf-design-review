Volunteer

Help us build our table

Juneteenth Conference is powered by volunteers who believe in celebrating Black excellence in tech. Join the team and help us deliver an unforgettable experience.

Volunteer Perks

★ Free conference pass
★ Exclusive volunteer t-shirt
★ Networking with speakers & attendees
★ Be part of something meaningful

Volunteer Roles

Registration & Check-In

Welcome attendees, manage badge distribution, and ensure a smooth arrival experience.

Session Support

Assist speakers with room setup, A/V needs, timekeeping, and audience Q&A.

Wayfinding & Hospitality

Guide attendees around the venue and help everyone feel welcome and oriented.

Social Media & Content

Capture photos, live-tweet sessions, and help share the conference experience online.

Ready to volunteer?

Send us a note and tell us a bit about yourself and how you'd like to help.
