Since 2020, Juneteenth Conference has brought together Black technologists for learning, celebration, and community. Here's a look back at our journey.

2023 In-Person & Virtual

Juneteenth Conference 2023

Our first in-person event, streamed to a virtual audience. Featured a keynote by Tim Banks (Developer Advocate, Dell) and a fireside chat with Minda Harts (Author of The Memo). 20 sessions across two days covering engineering, data science, security, and tech leadership.

Juneteenth Conference 2020

The inaugural Juneteenth Conference launched with 20 sessions across two days, drawing over 20,000 live viewers. Conceived and delivered in just two weeks, the event received coverage from Business Insider and showcased the depth and breadth of Black talent in tech.
