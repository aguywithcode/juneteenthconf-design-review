Our Programs

Three pillars of impact driving Black excellence in technology.

Juneteenth Conference

Annual two-day gathering featuring 20 curated sessions across engineering, data, security, entrepreneurship, and tech leadership. In-person in Chicago with an attendee dinner cruise party.

Tech Workforce Accelerator

Hands-on project experience, mentorship, and career pipeline support for Black professionals entering or advancing in tech. Real-world skills, real-world results.

Mentorship Program

Pairing aspiring technologists with experienced professionals for upskilling, portfolio building, and career guidance. Building lasting connections that drive careers forward.
